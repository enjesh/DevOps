    DevOps is a culture of collaboration between the Operation team
and the Development team within an organisation to increase speed of
delivering applications, ensure continous integration and reduce the
complexity of maintaining an application.

    The Operations and Development engineers participating together
in the entire service lifecycle, from design through the development 
process to production support.

    Software is developed, continously monitored, while maintaining
streamlined communication.

# DevOps is the union of people, process and products to enable continous delivery of value to end users.
# The goal is to bring teams together.
